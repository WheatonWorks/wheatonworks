Meet Naomi

Welcome to the vibrant world of Naomi's Floral Designs, where each petal is a stroke of creativity and every arrangement tells a unique story. Founded by Naomi Stewart, a passionate floral artist with an unwavering love for nature's beauty, our boutique floral studio is a celebration of artistry and botanical elegance.
Naomi discovered her flair for floral design at a young age, finding solace and inspiration in the intricate dance of colors and textures that flowers offer. What began as a hobby blossomed into a lifelong devotion, leading her to pursue formal training in floral design and horticulture.
Driven by a desire to share her artistic vision with the world, Naomi embarked on a journey to establish her own business, blending her expertise with a touch of entrepreneurial spirit. Each arrangement that bears her signature reflects not only her mastery of technique but also her keen eye for detail and unwavering commitment to excellence.
